target Audience

Our target audience is people who don't understand how to add a widget on their phone that shows them the weather forecast. Additionally, our target audience is interested in the juicy stuff going on around town.

individual 1:

individual 1 is joe baseball. He loves to travel, has kind of a lot of money and a closesly buzzed head. He likes to know what the weather will be like when he travels around idaho

individual 2:

individual 2 is Fudd Johnson. He claims not to like technology but plays stupid games on his phone all day. He wants to know what the weather's looking like for his hunting trips.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: joe baseball want to relive his glory days on a trip to preston idaho and wants to know what the weather will be like a week in advance

scenario 2: fudd johnson needs to get around town and doesn't know how to get to a map of the area but sees that there's a local map on the bottom of the page

scenario 3: fudd accidentally shot his buddy out hunting and want to see if the local press has picked up on it. Very usefull to see the local news on the weather website

scenario 4: Joe baseball wants to plan a cookout with his buds and he shares the website with them to reassure them the weather will be good.

scenario 5: Joe baseball is bored and wants to learn more about the area so he looks at the website to learn more.
